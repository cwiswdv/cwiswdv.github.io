About Me

I first got interested in programming in middle school, where I was exposed to things like Scratch and Code Combat. In high school, my interests shifted to graphic design; those assignments were more interesting, and the programming classes were too focused on websites for my taste. I went to college for graphic design at first, but I quickly grew a distaste for being forced to create art, so I circled back around to programming and picked up Python before studying for an Associate's Degree in Software Development at CWI.
